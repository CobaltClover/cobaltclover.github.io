A Day In My Life

Morning

I wake up at 8 am through the wonderful use of an phone made of silicon. I brush my teath for the recommended two minutes by dentists around the world. On my computer lays the tweet of some christian I flawlessly destroyed by asking them how do they know the Bible is true. I then make breakfast made of eggs that came from the chickens long ago.

Noonish

As I drive my commuite, I speed up my car to get past the line of pedestrians trying to cross. With seconds left to spare, I managed to clock in to the research lab before anyone notice I was missing. At this lab I study astronomy in order to find out what happen around the Big Bang.

Lateish

After a very eventful day where I printed out charts of the CBR (cosmic background radiation) and headed back home. I made some microwave dinner while I went on my phone to argue about the orgins of the universe until midnight. Afterwards I brushed my teeth again for two minutes and went to bed, ready to do the same thing the very next day.
